# Blog intake questions (default)

Use this when a user asks in prose for a new blog post and required inputs are missing.

## Required questions

1. What is the working title?
2. What is the primary keyword?
3. What angle do you want this to take?
4. Who is the target audience?
5. What is the business intent (awareness, consideration, or decision)?

## Optional questions

1. Should I include external research from web, Reddit, and Hacker News?
2. Do you want internal/external link minimums?
3. What export format do you want (markdown only, or markdown + CMS JSON)?

## Execution rule after intake

Once answers are provided, execute the full Deadwater skill chain immediately without asking the user to name skills.
